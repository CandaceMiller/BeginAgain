Here's a little about what we do :

Addiction often feels lonely, but it doesn't have to be. There are organizations in every community that want to connect you with the resources you need. We are here to point you in the right direction.
Most community organizations advocate for drug and alcohol prevention, while focusing on helping people in recovery maintain sobriety and confidentiality. Let us get you started!

We hope you find what you need !

Meetings
Almost every community in the United States hosts Alcoholics Anonymous, Narcotics Anonymous and other types of support meetings.
Many require you to begin a 12 Step Program, but several support groups adhere to other recovery models. Find the right support group for you.

Sponsers
Sobriety is easier with help. Healthy relationships with friends and family are vital to recovering and maintaining long-term sobriety. Social support plays a critical role in our sobriety!
You shouldn't hide in isolation because recovery is hard. Relationships with a sponser will help you through very stressful situations because they truly understand what you're going through.
